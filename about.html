About Us

Company History

ANI Generics is a research and development company with corporate origins in India The experiences of the people who founded ANIGenerics and established contacts in generic market were the basis of the expansion We focus on dossier development for multiple markets and develops globally accepted dossiers on exclusive basis.

Our Vision

To make a significant contribution to global generic basket by developing and Manufacturing high quality generics at affordable cost.

Our Mission

ANI Generics will contract develop and formulate pharmaceutical products for the global pharmaceutical industry in a form and manner that suits the diverse regulatory requirements of multiple markets. We will also strive to unlock value for the customer by creating a global dossier that is capable of replication across multiple markets with relative ease. .
